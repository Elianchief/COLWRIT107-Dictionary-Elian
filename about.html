About This Website
This dictionary outlines a branch of the Spanish language. El Salvador being a country rich in culture, this dictionary highlights about 56 slang words that are commonly used by the Salvadoran people.
Disclaimer: Most words have been translated from the website “Dikcionario” (linked below) which provided the definitions of the words. I made some adjustments to the translations on occasions I thought it would help to make it clearer in English. Although this is a Salvadoran dictionary, this doesn’t mean that all Salvadoran people have the same understanding/definition of the words. Even for Salvadorans, some words have different meanings depending on the region. I’ve tried to give the most accurate and general translation and meaning to each word.
